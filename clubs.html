TECHNICAL CLUBS OF IIITDM

Technical clubs at IIITDM Kancheepuram play a vital role in nurturing innovation and practical skills among students. These clubs focus on diverse areas such as coding, robotics, electronics, design, and entrepreneurship. They regularly organize workshops, hackathons, and competitions to encourage hands-on learning and collaboration. Through these activities, students get valuable exposure beyond academics, fostering creativity, teamwork, and a strong technical foundation.
